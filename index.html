Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an order take?

Custom orders typically take 5-7 business days for design, cutting, and finishing. Set designs may be faster (3-5 days).

Can I provide my own design?

Absolutely! We work closely with you to ensure your design is laser-ready and matches your vision. Send us your files during the enquiry process.

What materials do you use?

We primarily use high-quality woods (MDF, birch ply) and certain acrylics, depending on the product requirements.
