About Us

       

SK_Lasercuts was founded in May 2022.We are a **family owned business** that started with someone in need of          a gift but unable to find one in conventional shops. Our first product          was a wooden gift for a baby shower. What began as a side project for a          retired nurse has grown into a **quality-driven, well-known business**. We are passionate about our designs and take pride in our excellent customer service. SK_Lasercuts is based          in the southern suburbs of Cape Town.
